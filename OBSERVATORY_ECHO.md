## Implementation (minimal)

You already have `DECAY_MS`. Add a second stage:

- **Live trace** → full opacity, 12 s decay.
- After live decay → trail becomes **echo**: same points, much longer decay (e.g. 5 minutes), drawn with very low alpha, cold color.

No new data structures. Just: strong line → weak line → very slow fade.
