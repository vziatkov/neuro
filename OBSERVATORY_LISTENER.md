## Idea

When motion curves accumulate, the field **interprets them** — not as literal music at first, but as a **spatial score**. From each trajectory: speed, curvature, frequency of change, distance to others → **rhythm and harmony**. The system becomes **synesthetic**: movement becomes light, sound, vibration of space.

When three people move in sync → **harmony**. When trajectories are chaotic → **noise**. The field doesn’t just display; it **responds**.

## User feeling

Change trajectory → the field’s response changes. **Collective instrument**: like a theremin, a laser harp, an orchestra of motion.

## Why it’s unique

Data Arts often does: visualize music, or music from data. Here: **music emerges from collective human movement in real time**. Rare combo: **P2P + movement + emergent sound**.
