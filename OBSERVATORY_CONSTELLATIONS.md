# The Constellations

**Human motion becomes stars**

---

## Turn

Observatory shows curves, traces, mesh. The brain reads patterns best as **constellations**. Add one layer: **sometimes the space turns traces into constellations.**

When enough traces, echoes, and intersections accumulate, the field **connects points** — and a **constellation** appears: nodes at crossings, links between them, a geometric figure (like Orion, Ursa Major) but made by **human motion**.

It lives **~30 seconds**. Then lines dissolve, only echoes remain, space returns to chaos.
