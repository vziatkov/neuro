## Next: Curve Mesh, Resonance, Bloom

- **Curve mesh** — when curves intersect, a surface appears. Not just lines: *tissue of signal*.
- **Resonance** — when motions align (speed, direction, proximity), the space responds: glow, node, ripple.
- **The First Signal** — when 2–3 people move in sync, the field *blooms*: a rare, temporary structure (see OBSERVATORY_FIRST_SIGNAL.md). *Make the field bloom.*
- **The Echo** — when people leave, traces become faint *echoes* (long decay, pale, ghost). The field remembers (see OBSERVATORY_ECHO.md). *Every motion leaves an echo.*
- **The Constellations** — when enough traces and intersections accumulate, the field connects points into a *constellation* (like Orion, made by motion). Lasts ~30 s, then fades. *Leave a trace. Become a star.* (see OBSERVATORY_CONSTELLATIONS.md)
- **The Listener** — the field interprets movement as signal (speed, curvature, density → rhythm, harmony). Response: light, sound, vibration. *The field listens.* (see OBSERVATORY_LISTENER.md)
- **The Observer Effect** — when users stop moving and look, the field reacts to attention: the last curve stabilizes, structure crystallizes around it. *The field changes when it is seen.* (see OBSERVATORY_OBSERVER_EFFECT.md)
- **Identity Drift** — movement signature (speed, curvature, density) → spectral color per participant; color drifts with style. *Movement becomes identity.* (see OBSERVATORY_IDENTITY_DRIFT.md)
