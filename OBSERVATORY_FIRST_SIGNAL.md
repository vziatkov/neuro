# The First Signal

**When the field wakes up**

---

## Viral hook

The field **wakes up only when several people are present**.

- **1 person** → just a trail, silence.
- **2–3 people** → the field **reacts**: curves attract, mesh appears, a node forms.
- User realizes: *"The space is responding to us."*

Rare event: if **3 people move in sync** for a few seconds → **Signal Bloom** — flash, mesh structure, temporary “star”, pattern. Lasts ~10 s, then fades.

---

## Why it’s shareable

People start saying:

> “Open the link. Let’s try to make a bloom.”

Not “look at this” — **create an event**. Social goal without rules, UI, or score. Emergent behaviour: people sync, experiment, chase the moment.
